    While visiting your injured squadmate at Huerta Memorial Hospital, enter the room across the corridor from your squadmate's in order to overhear a salarian scientist having a comm conversation about the missing prototypes.
  • Bomb
    The turian team that crashed on Tuchanka was trying to prevent Cerberus from detonating a bomb that would cause massive casualties on the planet. Land on Tuchanka and help the turian platoon stop Cerberus.
